About US



OScode is a student-led open-source club at NMIT, fostering learning, collaboration, and innovation. We help students explore open-source contributions, work on real-world projects, and prepare for platforms like Google Summer of Code (GSoC). Our community brings like-minded developers together to learn, build, and grow through hands-on experience. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, OScode provides the perfect space to enhance your skills and advance your career.
